Artful Business Creations


About the project:

Artful Business Creation, commonly called ABC from THUAS students, is a project where students are asked to come up with a solution for a client using creative ways.

My team's client was "Young and Unlimited" a company based in Voorburg, that aims at reducing anxiety and burnouts among young people. Our mission was to ideate some new ways to involve youngsters into activities that would open their eyes on these delicate topics.

Our product:

After a lot of research and brainstorming, we decided to create a non-profit event around Voorburg as part of the school activities for children and teenagers. Participants would experience the different phases of self-discovery that the client offers.

Methodology:

Artful Business Creation is based on a very similar approach to what User Experience designers have familiarized with until now: Research, Converging, Diverging, How Might We questions, prototyping.

However, the name used for these stages in ABC are different. Its fundamentals can be found in a manual called Frame Creations, which helps taking business problems from a creative perspective.
